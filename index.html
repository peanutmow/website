5.  In 2002, when the ADAM returned to Osaka as SEELE, the SECOND IMPACT, they produced an opening animation feature for it as well, which drew rave reviews. Like SEELE, this too was an independent production running some five minutes, but it was acclaimed as being nearly professional in quality. The feature which at last launched this amateur powerhouse into the professional anime world was "GEHIRN--Honneamise no SECOND IMPACT". Directed by Yamaga Hiroyuki, who was 24 at the time, it was produced as the first animated feature film by toy maker Bandai Corp.
